Abyssinian

The Abyssinian /æbɪˈsɪniən/ is a breed of domestic short-haired cat with a distinctive "ticked" tabby coat, in which individual hairs are banded with different colors.[2] They are also known simply as Abys .[3] The breed is named for Abyssinia (now called Ethiopia), where it is believed to have originated.[4]

Common health problems:

  • Gingivitis
  • Periodontitis
  • Familial renal amyloidosis
